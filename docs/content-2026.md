## 5. Content Tone & Voice

**Target Audience**:

- Academic researchers (professors, postdocs)
- Graduate students (PhD, Masters)
- Industry professionals (engineers, architects)
- Mix of native and non-native English speakers

**Tone Guidelines**:

- **Professional** but approachable
- **Clear and concise** (international audience)
- **Welcoming** and inclusive
- **Informative** without being overwhelming
- **Action-oriented** (clear CTAs)

**Writing Style**:

- Short paragraphs (3-4 sentences max)
- Bullet points for lists
- Active voice preferred
- Simple sentence structure
- Avoid idioms or cultural references
- Define technical terms
- Use consistent terminology

**Avoid**:

- Overly academic language
- Marketing jargon
- Culturally-specific references
- Ambiguous dates (use international format: DD Month YYYY)
- Assumptions about prior knowledge
